About Me

I am Colt McManis, and I am a graphic designer. I was raised in southern Ohio, and I grew up obsessed with sports. I was infatuated with not only the elements of the game but also with what went into creating a successful team.

Currently, I am a senior at Ohio University majoring in graphic design with a minor in marketing. Throughout my undergrad career, I have worked as a graphic designer for Ohio University Football while also working part-time as a retail color consultant with my local Sherwin Williams paint store. This past summer, I interned as a graphic designer for the St. Louis Surge, a women's professional basketball team. Through my professional experience as a graphic designer, marketing minor, and color consultant, I have emboldened strengths in adaptability, communication, creativity, and precision. 

Every day of my life provides me with the opportunity to learn. All of the moments in my jobs, classes, and conversations come together to strengthen not only my collaboration skills and work ethic, but also my ability as a graphic designer. These skills have assisted in leading me to where I am now, where I have the privilege of combining design with my passion for sports. The moments that transcend the game itself are what create lasting memories for the fans, and these memories follow us for the rest of our lives. Whether it is sparking a conversation or something to reminisce on, they are truly what keep us together. For me, it's not just about creating visually appealing designs; it's about capturing the essence of those moments and translating them into resonant visuals.

My journey through the world of sports so far has allowed me to blend my passion with every project. As a designer, I not only have the opportunity to represent a team with my creativity and precision but also be an integral part of the moments fans will carry with them forever. The moments that, when I was growing up, led me to where I am now.
